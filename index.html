A place that holds your group.

Our practice hall was built out of old oak and intention. The floors have been smoothed by bare feet, and the large windows let the morning light spill in perfectly for your first session.

The rooms are quiet and simple, each one a little different. We believe sleep is a huge part of the work you do here, so we've placed great focus on proper beds and good pillows.

Just 10 minutes from Rogaška Slatina, we are completely surrounded by fields. The chickens roam free, the goats are friendly, and the air smells like dirt and pine. It feels like returning home.
